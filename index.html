Musical Hands

Welcome


Musical hands is an interactive website where the visitor can play a virtual instrument only with this hands.

Music is played most of the times with the hands, so why can’t we use it with your webcam to make music? This is what Musical Hands gives you.., the chance to experiment and play around with the website, interacting with your hands, playing sounds and allowing you to discover the space of your screen.

How it works

Instructions


To begin using it, you need to allow access to your webcam in your browser. Click "allow” when asked if you want to give permission to access it.

Right click on the button "click here!"

Scroll down until you see the video of your camera and start moving your hand throughout the screen to begin triggering sounds.

Get familiarized with the locations of the sounds in your screen and start improvising as you wish!

Note: It is better if your face is out of the sight of the camera, so the system can properly track only your hand.

Let's make some music together!

Enjoy!
